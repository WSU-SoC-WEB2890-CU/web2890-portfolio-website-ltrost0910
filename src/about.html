About Me

After a 35+ year career as a developer and manager specializing in enterprise systems, backend processing, and core business applications, I decided to return to Weber State to further explore web design and development. Building on my WSU Information Technology bachelor's degree, I have now earned a Web Essentials Certificate and am nearing the completion of an AAS in Web and User Experience.

Now, I enjoy taking one class per semester as a retirement hobby, and applying my new web skills to design, develop and maintain websites for local groups and small businesses. Check out samples of my work by viewing my Portfolio page!
